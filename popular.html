Popular Attractions

East Park

Water slides, a water park, an arcade, bumper cars, rock climbing, go carts, mini golf, batting cages, driving range, and a jungle gym, East Park is a dream for any kid! The perfect location for birthday parties!

Learn More

Museum London

Museum London is the spot for visual art and material culture. Within are over 5,000 regional and Canadian works of art as well as 45,000 historical artifacts that reflect the history of London!

Learn More

Richmond Row

With over 200 businesses catering to your every need, Richmond Row is one of the most popular places in London. It is also full of night life with multiple bars on the strip for those fun nights out!

Learn More

Covent Garden Market

Covent Garden Market is renowned for its fresh produce, gourmet foods, unique gifts, Public Square, family programming, and seasonal outdoor farmers' Market! It even has an ice rink outside during the winter months!
